About

Currently I'm a intern research fellow at the European Space Agency. My research interests include satellite image processing, data fusion, image segmentation and deep learning. The applications of my research is centered on environment monitoring.

Bio

Previously I was a PhD Candidate at Université Bretagne Sud where I was part of the mathematics laboratory LMBA working with Pr. Jacques Froment and also part of the Obelix team at IRISA lab where I worked with Pr. Nicolas Courty . My PhD was a partership with the University of Balearic Islands, Spain, where I was supervised by Pr. Tomeu Coll and collaborated with Dr. Joan Duran . During my PhD I worked on developping mathematicals models for satellite image processing, namely hyperspectral and multispectral image fusion. For that, I used the theory of optimal transport and non-local variational models.
